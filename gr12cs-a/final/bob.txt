shrine of its revolutionary leader.</p><p itemprop="articleBody">Mizan Online, which is affiliated with the judiciary, attributed the toll from Wednesday&#x2019;s attacks to Pirhossein Kolivand, the head of Iran&#x2019;s emergency department.</p><p itemprop="articleBody">Several gunmen and suicide bombers attacked parliament and the shrine to Ayatollah Ruhollah Khomeini, setting off an hours-long siege at the legislature that ended with four attackers dead. The Islamic State group claimed the attacks.</p><p itemprop="articleBody">It marks the first attack in Iran claimed by the extremist group, which is at war with Iranian-backed forces in Syria and
